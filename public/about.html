My Story

My journey into programming began during high school, when I was introduced to coding by my cousin—a skilled software developer. He showed me how websites are built and what could be achieved through code. I was immediately fascinated and asked him to teach me the basics. That early exposure sparked a passion that has only grown stronger over time.

Driven by curiosity, I began self-learning through online courses and tutorials, diving deep into web development and computer science fundamentals. I soon realized that this wasn’t just a hobby—it was the career path I wanted to pursue. That decision led me to enroll in the Computer Science & Technology Diploma program at Langara College in Canada.

While advancing academically, I also explored the world of business. I built and managed several dropshipping stores and learned the ins and outs of digital marketing, Facebook Ads, sales strategy, and client communication. These experiences shaped me not only as a developer, but also as a communicator, problem solver, and entrepreneur.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege to work on a wide range of technical projects—from responsive websites to complex backend systems, mobile applications, AI experiments, and even open-source contributions. Each project challenged me to grow and reinforced my belief in continuous learning and innovation.

When I’m not writing code, I’m likely reading about emerging tech, collaborating with fellow developers, or mentoring others who are just starting their journey. I believe in community, collaboration, and building technology that creates real impact in people’s lives.
